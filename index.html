About Me

I was born and grew up in Columbia, S.C for most of my life. I went to Hammond School from Elementary thru High School. After High School, I found a company called "Projects Abroad" and ended up living in Southern Africa, mainly Botswana, helping with conservation research with Lions and Elephants. After that incredible experience, I chose to return to the States to study but shortly decided to drop out and move to Jackson Hole, WY.

After a couple years spent in Jackson Hole, I quickly realized that availible jobs and job security where very hard to come by in an extremely small town. I worked as a Snowplow Driver, a Bartender, Landscaper, Bowling Mechanic, for the airport handling bags, Sales at a retail store... after getting my EMT and Wilderness First Responder...

As time went by and I continued to age with no real career path I decided to look back to schooling to make myself more marketable and have a vague path laid out for my future career.
